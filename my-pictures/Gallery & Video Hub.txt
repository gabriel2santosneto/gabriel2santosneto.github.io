My Pictures — Gallery & Video Hub
==================================

My Pictures is the multimedia section, housing visual content like robotics projects, camps, classroom shots, and events. It acts as a gallery window with image folders and embedded videos.

Subfolders:
-----------
• Robotics Projects — LEGO SPIKE, VEX, Sphero builds.
• STEM Camps — Activity highlights and team photos.
• Classroom Activities — Students coding and building.
• 3D Printing & Design — 3D models and final prints.
• Events & Exhibitions — Fairs, showcases, and teacher workshops.
• Media Highlights.txt — Captions and special mentions.

Hosting:
--------
For performance, galleries can link to Google Drive albums or embedded YouTube/Drive videos.
