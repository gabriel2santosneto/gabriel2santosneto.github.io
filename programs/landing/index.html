What is the client process?
▼
We start with a discovery call to understand your requirements and how my services can help. After our call, I'll send a contract and proposal. Once agreed, you'll be onboarded and gain access to our project management system, where everything is managed. Client communication happens via Zoom, email, or your preferred platform.
How much do you price your services at?
▼
Each service and project is priced differently based on its complexity and the amount of work required. This is discussed during our discovery call.
How long does it take to complete a project?
▼
Each project typically takes around 1–2 weeks to complete, but this depends on its complexity and the client's response time.
